    Trigon has destroyed Azarath and the Titans are reflecting on the battle that they lost. Determined to save Raven, they seek for help, but Arella and Azarath refused to provide it.     • The Streets of Trigon's Planet

    The Titans are urgently trying to escape when they run into his dog, Fang; they leave Beast Boy to deal with him. Their plan is to combine their powers in order to send Trigon to the Netherverse. It works, but at the last minute, Arella jumps in after Trigon before the portal explodes. Raven reveals that in order to keep Trigon from escaping, someone had to block his path and that person was Arella.

This is a two semester project searching through and finding out information from the Teen Titans television series. The goal in the first semester was to compare the original Teen Titans series with the comic books. We approached this through marking up the Season One transcripts found at the Teen Titans Episode Wiki and viewing the comics online. The goal in the second semester was to create a map of Jump City and track the routes of the five lead characters of the tv series: Robin, Cyborg, Starfire, Raven and Beast Boy. We chose to mark up the Second Season of the series, again found here.
